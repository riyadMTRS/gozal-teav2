Our Story

Gozal Tea is a tribute to the awakening of a new day. Founded in the heart of the UAE, our mission is to capture the magic of sunrise in every leaf, every cup, every moment.

We journey to the misty highlands of Sri Lanka, the emerald slopes of Darjeeling, and the sun-drenched meadows of Fujian—handpicking only the freshest, most vibrant teas. Our Emirati heritage inspires us to blend these treasures with saffron, dates, and indigenous botanicals, creating a signature flavor as radiant as the desert dawn.

With Gozal, tea is not just a drink. It’s a sunrise, a celebration, and an invitation to savor serenity and vitality.
